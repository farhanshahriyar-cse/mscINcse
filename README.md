## 📖 About This Repository

This repository is a **community-driven academic resource** for students of **BSc CSE** and **MSc CSE** at HSTU. It contains organized collections of:

- 📝 Quiz Question Papers
- 📋 Assignment Question Sheets
- 📄 Mid-Term & Final Exam Question Papers
- 📚 Course Materials & Lecture Notes
- 🔬 Research Papers & Project Reports

All materials are organized **semester-wise and session-wise** for easy access.

## 🤝 Contribute to This Repository

> **Are you a BSc or MSc CSE student or alumni of HSTU with question papers, quiz sheets, or study materials not yet in this repository?**

Your contribution helps **hundreds of fellow students** at HSTU!

### How to Contribute

1. **Fork** this repository
2. **Add** your materials (PDFs) to the relevant folder
3. **Submit a Pull Request (PR)** with a short note on what you added
4. Your PR will be **reviewed and merged** ✅

### We Welcome

- 📄 Exam & quiz papers from **any year or session**
- 📋 Assignment question sheets
- 🎓 **BSc CSE** question papers — any semester or year
- 🔄 Missing sessions of already listed MSc CSE courses
- 📚 Lecture notes and study materials
